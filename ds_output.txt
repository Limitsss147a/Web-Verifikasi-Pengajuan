### Key Effects
Clear focus rings (3-4px), ARIA labels, skip links, responsive design, reduced motion, 44x44px touch targets

### Avoid (Anti-patterns)
- Ornate design
- Low contrast
- Motion effects
- AI purple/pink gradients

- [ ] Focus states visible for keyboard nav
- [ ] prefers-reduced-motion respected
